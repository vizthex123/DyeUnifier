# Dye Recipe Unifier Changelog

A full changelog for every version (and sub-version) of the [Datapack](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/data-packs/dye-recipe-unifier).

This one will be updated more often than the CurseForge and Modrinth changelogs.

I increment the version by X.1 whenever I add more mod support (so long as the addition has 8+ recipes), and X.X.1 for minor fixes and/or additions I missed in the last patch.

Lines appended with a version in brackets (e.g. Text Here [1.19]) denote changes that only apply to that version.

--------------------------------------------------

## 4.1

All Coral types are now smelting recipes<br />
Aquamirae's Wisteria Niveis is now a double green dye item<br />
Updated support for Alex's Caves, End's Phantasm, Forestry, and Jaden's Nether Expansion<br />
Changed Melon Slices to use custom recipes to prevent conflicts with vanilla's Slice -> Seed recipe<br />
Fixed a few other taggable items not being tagged that I forgot to list here

<br />

Added support for Create's millstone
- Thankfully, most of the mods this pack supports are already covered - so I mostly added recipes for a few items that were missed, as well as a couple of the niche mods Create's built-in compat doesn't cover.
- My custom Centrifugal Seperator recipes are also there, but they take longer to process than a standard recipe
